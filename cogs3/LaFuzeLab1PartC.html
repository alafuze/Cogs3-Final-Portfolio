Making Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Pasta sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Water
  • Parmesean cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water and a drizzle of olive oil to a boil (olive oil prevents noodles from sticking together)
  2. Once boiling, add the spaghetti noodles to the water and let cook for about 10 minutes
  3. Strain the noodles and set aside
  4. In a sauce pan, heat your pasta sauce of choice
  5. Once heated, add the strained spaghetti noodles to the sauce
  6. Stir until combined
  7. Add parmesean cheese on top and serve!
